Interactive data story about the changes in purchasing power and the value of money in Finland

Inflation started to speed up during 2022 and 2023. We wanted to find out what it actually meant for Finnish people in comparison to price changes in the last 50 years. The result is a data-driven, interactive article that changes when the readers enter different salaries to our calculator. The infrographics won an award in The European Newspaper Award in 2023.
